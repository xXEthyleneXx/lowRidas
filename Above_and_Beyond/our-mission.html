What is Above and Beyond IT Solutions All About?
How it all began...
Owner and founder John Rhem began his official career in IT almost 15 years ago; however, even before that he was highly interested in computers and electronics. Having often worked on some unusual requests from friends to come up with out of the box solutions for merging electronics and devices, John soon realized his gift for adapting technology to best fit the needs of it's user. Also, beginning his IT career as a PC/LAN technician for the Georgetown Steel Mill he learned just what limits the average personal computer could be pushed to and what they were truly capable of. At the mill, John honed his skills as a technician, learning the finer art of customer satisfaction and the appreciation of a client / customer when you go above and beyond.
After leaving the Steel Mill in 2003 due to a massive layoff, John began the journey that would ultimately lead him to become a Network Administrator where he would serve that role for two companies over five years. From his early start working in the dust and debris of the mill on desktop computers to his later years at a successful financial firm involved with terabytes of data flowing across a network that spanned the entire state of South Carolina; John has lived and breathed IT.
Our mission, here at Above and Beyond IT Solutions is to provide the best possible service and a solutionthat meets our client's needs. Whether you're the owner of a large business with a growing network of computers and devices, or you're having a problem with your home computer we will strive to meet your solutions needs.
